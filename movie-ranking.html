The Best Movies According to Shumaila

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Seven Pounds

One of my favourite movie. This is an emotional and thriller movie. Tim Thomas played by Will Smith caused a fatal car crash while texting and driving, resulting in the deaths of seven people, including his fiancee, Sarah Jenson. To make amends, Tim embarks on a mission to save the lives of seven deserving individuals.

Moana

Really interesting anime.Moana, the chief's daughter, joins forces with the demigod Maui to explore a legendary island. Throughout their journey, they face challenges hidden by the ocean, discovering the importance of determination and belief in achieving their goals.

The Pursuit of Happyness

Super beautiful film. Really motivational.The movie is based on the true story of Christopher Paul Gardner, played by Will Smith, and it depicts his journey from rags to riches. It highlights his determination to overcome poverty and endure challenging circumstances without giving up.
